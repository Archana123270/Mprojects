Description

Barcelona is the largest city in Catalonia and has over a dozen Antoni Gaudí installations scattered throughout. The most famous being Sagrada Familia and Park Güell, this city is perfect for any fan of Catalan modernism.

Features

The outstanding Sagrada Familia Barcelona located in the city of Barcelona remains the most visited attraction in Spain. A world heritage site, the Sagrada Familia attracts over three million visitors each year. It is one of the most popular single attractions in the world.
